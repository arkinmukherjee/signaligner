Researchers want to be able to measure the physical activity, sedentary behavior, and sleep of the population.

New wearable devices, like smartwatches, track the daily motions of the millions of people who wear them.

Studying the motions unique to different people and activities can lead to breakthroughs in our understanding of disease and health.

In the future, your smartwatch may be able to predict what you are doing from the way you move. To make this possible, researchers need a lot of labeled data that matches activities to the signals collected from wearable devices.

This is where we need your help!
